The last time I was a student, it was 10 years ago. Had I dreamt of hosting a website for myself that moment, it would have been next to impossible. For, to be able to afford fiancially or find the right resources to be able to do it.

Today, there are so many wonderful organizations throughout the world who are coming up and helping not just students but everyone equip with technology for all of us to be able to architect new technology solutions

This page is a special mention for all tools that I was able to use to build this website. If you are looking to get yourself started with a website or learn the technologies behind them, these tools might be handy to you.

But my deepest gratitude goes to Github for their Education Pack. They are really awesome. If you are a student, I strongly recommend it

Github Education Pack

Github partnered with organizations and created an education pack which students can take advantage of. There are tons of tech available for free mostly for students. You need to register for the education pack and it would probably take couple of hours to get verified and you can start using them

Name.com

Name.com from Github Education Pack is service which offers domain name for registration. If you are a student and have registered in the pack, you get to register a domain of your choice there. Name.com is one among multiple other service providers offereing domain name registration. Visit the pack to know more details about it

Simple Analytics

I used Simple Analytics to visualize analytics for this site. This product is also from the education pack from Github and is really amazing and simple. The privacy friendly service is really amazing and is a great opportunity to explore analytics tools

Freehostia

I used Freehostia to host the domain. They were very kind to offer a plan that is free and have many services that you can make use of. You could set your nameservers to Freehostia and they have the capability to edit DNS in Freehostia to host your other products elsewhere

Let's Encrypt

After you host your website, it is important to install SSL Certificate and enable HTTPS redirection to help your visitors safely browse your website. I used Let's Encrypt to get a free SSL Certificate

Favicon Generator

Do you see a small red ribbon on the tab of this website on your browser? That's a favicon and I used favicon generator to generate a class of favicons to be displayed

Icons 8

Icons 8 helped with provide icons that I could use for the website. If you are subscribed with the Education Pack, Icons 8 would help you provide license to use their icons. I used a icon from their service for the favicon of this site

Zoho Mail

Thanks to Zoho Mail, I was able to host my email for this domain. Having an email associated with your domain gives you a professional look and it is fun getting things together. I had to configure the my hosting DNS to point to Zoho Mail to be able to retreive and send emails
